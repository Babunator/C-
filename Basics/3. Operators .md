# Arithmetic Operators
You can perform common mathematical operations with arithmetic operators.<br/>
|Operator|Name|Example|
|--------|-----|------|
|+|Addition|x + y|
|-|Subtraction|x - y|
|*|Multiplication|x * y|
|/|Division|x / y|
|%|Modulus|x % y|
|++|Increment|++x|
|--|Decrement|--x|
